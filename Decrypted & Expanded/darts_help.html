Rules:

1. Players take turns throwing a set of 3 darts at the board.

2. Each area of the board represents a specific score, plus the following bonuses:

3. To win, you must reduce your score to exactly zero.

4. If your total score gets below zero - it's called a BUST. BUST results in losing your turn while your score gets reset to the way it was before your turn.

5. If you reduce your score to zero before your opponent and you were the player to make the first turn - then your opponent will have a last chance to draw the game.

Goal:

The goal of the game is to reduce your score to 0 before your opponent.
